Honeybees

An autobiography on honeybees

Hello, My name is Harry

I am a honeybee.

I am 10 years old.

But...I still have to do work!

....that is collecting nectar

I wake up in the morning

And then I go out to collect nectar with my parents.

But it is not easy to do my work during the winter.

Because of snowfall we have trouble finding flowers for nectar

We return back to the honeycomb by sun set.

We stay in the honeycomb until the next morning
